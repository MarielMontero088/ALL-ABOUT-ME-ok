About Myself

  • MARIEL ANTONETTE U. MONTERO
  • June 13, 2005
  • LAWANIT VILLAGE, Barangay 7 (Pob.), NASIPIT, Agusan del Norte

I'm MARIEL ANTONETTE U. MONTERO, a dedicated student by day, delves into the captivating world of Mobile Legends by night. With textbooks replaced by strategy guides, he navigates the complexities of both academia and the virtual battlefield. In class, he daydreams of perfecting his hero skills, and during breaks, he passionately discusses game strategies with fellow enthusiasts. His backpack bears the subtle marks of a gamer's lifestyle a mobile charger, snacks for sustained energy during intense matches, and a notebook filled with hero sketches. For MARIEL ANTONETTE, the corridors of education and the realms of Mobile Legends seamlessly intertwine, creating a unique narrative of a student who aspires to be a hero in more ways than one.
